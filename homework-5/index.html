Baden-Württemberg

Baden-Württemberg isa landin the southwestof Germany. The capital is the city ofStuttgart.

It was formed on 25 April 1952 by the unification of the western federal states of Württemberg-Baden , South Baden and Württemberg-Hohenzollern , which were under the occupation of England , France and the United States . Baden-Württemberg is the state with the highest level of exports (2019), with a low unemployment rate and the fourth highest gross domestic product ( GDP ) per capita (2020). In terms of life expectancy, both for men and women , Baden-Württemberg ranked first among the federal states of Germany for the period 2018-2020 . In terms of population and area, Baden-Württemberg ranks third among the states of Germany.

The state consists of a union of several historical states of the German region of Swabia : Baden , Hohenzollern and Württemberg .After World War II, the Allies created three states: Württemberg-Hohenzollern , South Baden (both occupied by France ), and Württemberg-Baden (occupied by the United States ). In 1949, these three states became part of the Federal Republic of Germany . Article 118 of the FRG constitution allowed the three regions to unite. Following a plebiscite on 9 December 1951 , these regions were united on 25 April 1952 to form the state of Baden-Württemberg.
